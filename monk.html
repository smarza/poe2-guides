Core route identity

The run is not Siphoning Strike from level 1. It is a staged quarterstaff campaign:

Act 1: Power Charge burst + Frozen Locus Late Act 1: Frost Bomb + Tempest Bell + lightning bridge Act 2: Invoker + Storm Wave + weapon shopping Act 2 end onward: Siphoning Strike shock/crit core

What makes or breaks the run

The build is flexible, but the pace depends on four recurring constraints: quarterstaff upgrades, shock uptime, not overplaying clunky combos, and enough life/defense to survive before burst lands.

Most important rule: check vendors for quarterstaffs every level or two. A good vendor staff can almost double DPS.

Primary objective

Do not face-tank packs. Isolate enemies, generate Power Charges, spend them for burst, and add Frozen Locus + Frost Bomb before relying on long combos. Finish Act 1 with shock pathing and the lightning/talisman bridge ready.

Do this session

  • Fight one-on-one when possible; Monk is fragile early.
  • Craft Frozen Locus and use it to split dangerous packs.
  • Add Rage to main attack, then Frost Bomb and Tempest Bell.
  • Kill the mushroom boss for the support gem → Rapid Attacks.
  • Path toward chance to shock even if it looks odd early.
  • After the major boss, buy the cheap talisman and pick Herald of Thunder.

Early gear priorities

PriorityWhat to look for
1Quarterstaff upgrade: increased physical damage.
2Life on armor and accessories.
3Resistances when needed; do not ignore defense.
4Damage prefixes on weapons over bad suffixes.
5Cheap talisman/offhand after Act 1 boss for lightning spike.

Failure point to avoid

The lightning/talisman bridge is powerful but button-heavy. Do not get stuck perfecting every combo in Act 1 — it is a temporary phase until Siphoning Strike replaces it.

Do this session

  • Check vendors every level; Act 2 staff upgrades can double damage.
  • Craft Storm Wave at level 22 and complete Trial of Sekhemas.
  • Choose Invoker: shock magnitude, 10% damage as extra lightning, shocked ground.
  • When level 8 gem drops, craft Siphoning Strike with Elemental Armament + Pinpoint Critical.
  • Drop Tempest Bell/talisman dependency once the new loop feels strong.

The Siphoning Strike switch

Around 01:50:12, the run changes permanently. Shock enemy → Siphoning Strike consumes shock → shocked ground / repeated hits reapply shock → repeat. Invoker makes this spammable.

Ascendancy direction

Invoker shock scaling is why the final build works. Shocked ground keeps enemies shocked so Siphoning Strike can be used repeatedly without awkward charge juggling.

Primary objective

Run the simplified shock → Siphoning Strike loop. Add boss tools (Sniper Mark, Infernal Cry), upgrade weapon to Slicing Quarterstaff, and finish Trials of Chaos.

Do this session

  • Drop Ice Strike / Freezing Mark if they slow you down.
  • Add Sniper Mark for crit scaling on bosses.
  • Take the extra spirit/book route in Infested Barrens.
  • Invest in crit chance, crit with Power Charges, and enough life/defense.
  • Prefer Wind Dancer over Lingering Illusion if defense is shaky.
  • Skip Mantra of Destruction — treated as unreliable in this route.

Gear priorities

PriorityWhat to look for
1Slicing Quarterstaff or better phys/crit/ele staff.
2Critical hit chance / damage on weapon and tree.

4Elemental damage and Power Charge scaling.
5Accuracy if Siphoning Strike misses often.

Defense check

Damage spikes hard in Act 3, but Monk stays fragile. Stack stun threshold, life, and Wind Dancer if needed. Most deaths happen before burst lands, not because damage is too low.

Primary objective

Finish Act 4 efficiently. Add Exposure Cry, scale crit, and reach Tawakai at level 49 minimum (prefer 50). Do not fall too far below zone level even while rushing.

Do this session

  • Create Exposure Cry when support gem level 4 is available.
  • Instill amulet with Envy / Paranoia if the base is worth keeping.
  • Use crit shrines and trial rewards for burst windows.
  • Keep shock uptime → Siphoning Strike as default boss loop.
  • Fight Tawakai only when leveled; phased bosses limit one-shots.

Phased boss note

One-shots are less visible on multi-phase or protected bosses. That is mechanics, not missing damage. Stay on level curve if boss kills feel inconsistent.

Primary objective

Do Act 2 interlude first for life bonus, passive points, and levels. Upgrade weapon when level requirements allow — do not craft too early on gear you cannot equip yet.

Do this session

  • Route Kar Pazar interlude for life, passives, and levels.
  • Upgrade the level 54 quarterstaff from Tawakai when equippable.
  • Grab the skill book; continue crit + Power Charge investment.
  • Keep defense patched — still the biggest weakness late campaign.

End state

By ~04:17:57, the build demonstrates huge burst on strong content. Upgrade the quarterstaff further and the same shock → Siphoning Strike loop melts later progression.
