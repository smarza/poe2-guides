Core route identity

Every phase exists to generate Power Charges faster for Falling Thunder bursts:

Act 1 early: Killing Palm + Frozen Locus bridge Act 1 late: Wing Blast + Wind Blast stun engine Act 2: Storm Wave + Siphoning Strike + Hollow Palm Act 3–4: Perpetual Charge nukes + ES/evasion pivot

What makes or breaks the run

Hit the same power spikes, not every early experiment. Four constraints: talisman in weapon set 2, full armor Acts 1–3, shock before Falling Thunder, and do not pick Monk for first level-3 gem.

Most important rule: Wing Blast + Wind Blast is a bridge — drop Frozen Locus once stun clear works, then replace zone clear with Storm Wave loop in Act 2.

Primary objective

Reach the Wing Blast / Wind Blast stun engine before mid Act 1 ends. Equip talisman in weapon set 2, swap to Devour + Herald, and finish around level 14–15 with full armor stacked.

Do this session

  • Grab Frozen Locus from Clearfell camp — drop it once stun combo works.
  • Spend first level-3 gem on Wing Blast (Druid), not a Monk skill.
  • Equip any talisman in weapon set 2 before casting Wing Blast.
  • Move Rage + Rapid Attacks to Wind Blast; add Brink + Impact Shockwave.
  • After Executioner, replace Killing Palm with Devour.
  • Socket Herald of Thunder — everything from here stacks shock.

Early gear priorities

PriorityWhat to look for

Failure point to avoid

Do not heavy-stun with Wind Blast — it primes stun but cannot over-stun, keeping enemies ready for Wing Blast. Do not pick a Monk level-3 gem before Wing Blast.

Primary objective

Craft Storm Wave + Siphoning Strike at tier 7. Path to Hollow Palm Technique, unequip quarterstaff, and run shock extraction as default clear and boss loop.

Do this session

  • Storm Wave: Rage + Shock to 100% (+ Living Lightning in Act 3).
  • Siphoning Strike: Rapid Attacks + Charge Profusion.
  • Stop Frost Bomb; move Wing Blast to Devour for faster corpse dives.
  • Take shock-reapply-on-consume passive before Jaman' Rai.
  • Stack +melee skills on gloves/amulet after Hollow Palm.

The shock extraction switch

Storm Wave shocks → Siphoning Strike steals shock for Power Charges + shockwave → Falling Thunder burst. Repeat 2–3× per boss, or Wing Blast a stunned target for instant 3 charges.

Primary objective

Multi-cast Falling Thunder with Perpetual Charge. Confirm shock is on boss before bursting after Siphoning Strike extraction. Respec mana nodes after Clarity for lightning damage + penetration.

Do this session

  • Perpetual Charge on FT; Living Lightning on Storm Wave (not Branching Fissures).
  • Gorge + Thrill of the Kill on Devour for flask/life sustain.
  • Skip Lingering Illusion — charges are always available.
  • Before FT on bosses: verify shock still applied; extra Storm Wave if not.

Boss shock check

After extracting shock with Siphoning Strike, shock may not reapply. Do another Storm Wave before Falling Thunder — charges scale damage, shock multiplies it.

Primary objective

Begin ES/evasion gear on body armor. Swap sustain auras to Ghost Dance. The Falling Thunder shock loop carries through campaign end and into maps without mandatory Whirling Assault.

Do this session

  • Path passives toward ES, evasion, and tankiness.
  • Elemental Focus + Elemental Armament on Herald of Thunder.
  • Whirling Assault + Martial Artist ghosts — optional boss flair only.
  • Mobalytics Act 4 tab shows optional Whirling swap — narrative route keeps FT core.

Act 4 gear rule

Prioritize evasion/ES body for Hollow Palm attack speed and crit scaling. Do not drop talisman from weapon set 2.
